Property

Whether it be a place to live all year round or just a place for the summer then the Algarve is definately the right place to look. With new houses, villas and apartments being built every year the you will certainly have no shortage of choices when it comes to purchasing that dream place in the sun. Along with these new properties springing up there are also older properties that go on sale from time to time.
