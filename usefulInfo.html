Car Rental

Faro airport is the primary airport for the Algarve. For this reason it offers many choices for car rentals whether it be a big,small,black or red car that you prefer you are sure to find the right one from the many car rental companys available at the airport.

Bike Rental

Bike tours are a very popular activity in the Algarve. They are a perfect way to relax and take in the sights that the algrave has to offer. Bringing your bike overseas is not an easy task, luckily there many companies throughout the Algarve that will provide you with bikes to rent for as long as you need them.

Golf club Rental

With the Algarve being one of the worlds top golf destinations many people vist needing a set of golf clubs. Renting your golf clubs when you go on holiday is a great way to save on the costs of travelling with your clubs. Top quality golf clubs are available to rent at most golf courses or through external companies.
