Beaches

If beaches are what you are looking for then the algarve is truely the place to be. The stunning weather makes a trip to the algarve a must for anyone who enjoys the feeling of warm sand between their toes. The beaches of the Algarve are the perfect spot for anyone who enjoys water sporst or any of the other many beach activities. Being situated along the atlantic the Algarve is home to some of the most stunning cliff formations in the world which make for some spectacular sights for beach goers.

Water Parks

With its stunning tropical climate the algarve is the perfect place to take part in water activities and where better to do this than in a water park. Spending the day at one the Algarves many water parksis sure to be a fun day for all involved. If water parks arent your thing then maybe an aqua show is what you are looking for. The Zoomarine theme park is home to many exotic sea creatures and you can go here marvel at their beauty and watch the shows.

Nature Walks

The western and southern coasts of the Algarve are filled with stunning views of towering cliffs which look out over the vast and beautiful Atlantic ocean. People come from far and wide to walk along these cliffs and take in these amazing views. Along with these amazing views the Algarve, because of its proximity to Africa is home to many exoitic plants and wildlife. Where better to take in this amazing wildlife than in one of the Algarves many wildlife parks and nature reserves.
